github.com/dragonshifty/Catmodities

Catmodities

Catmodities is a cat-themed trading game with a simulated stock exchange. It features price crashes and rises, and story-based events drawing on famous cats throughout film, TV, and literature. Earn your way up to purchase bigger and better houses and open up new trading possibilities in the process. Utilises JavaFX, CSS, and music of my own composition.

github.com/dragonshifty/HowDidDavesDayGo

How Did Dave's Day Go?

Using design patterns to fully simulate a pizza restaurant, one waiter has his first day and experiences stress throughout the day depending on things/peoples/management he likes and dislikes. Each run is unique and you only find out whether he had a good day or bad day; follow the code to read the story. It also tells you if anyone was of ill in case it was someone he really likes.
